Data Upload Instructions
------------------------

This guide explains how to upload data files for processing. Please follow these steps:

Step 1: Upload Practitioner and Hospital Information
First, upload the support information files in the Step1_uploads folder.
	practitionerInformation.json
	hospitalInformation.json
These files contain essential practitioner and hospital data.

Step 2: Upload Patient FHIR Bundles
Next, upload the patient FHIR bundles in the Step2_uploads folder.
All files with the .json extension in this folder contain patient and related fhir data.

More synthetic patient data can be created with Synthea @ https://github.com/synthetichealth/synthea
